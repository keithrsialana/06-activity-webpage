Keith Sialana

Keith Sialana has been involved with computers ever since he could remember. His pursuit to becoming a Software Developer started since high-school and continues to be his dream career whilst having taught himself a lot of Desktop Technician skills.

Keith attended at Conestoga College in 2019 initially under the Software Engineer program, but transferred over to Computer Programmer/Analyst after his first semester. After the second year of Computer Programmer/Analyst, Keith took a 1 year co-op position with Linamar Corp.; The first 8 months as a Desktop Support Technician, and the last 4 months as an Application Developer. Afterwards, Keith graduated the college program in 2022 with an College Advanced Diploma with Distinction and an overall 3.7 GPA.
